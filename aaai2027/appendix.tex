\section{Supplementary Evidence Map}

The main paper promotes only the shortest evidence path: direct trained-proposer
CarCircle action audits, logged episode accumulation, and pre-registered
fixed-cap closed-loop audits. This supplement records the completed supporting
experiments that do not fit in the seven-page body. All rows use frozen query
blocks or pre-registered online audit traces; per-seed CSV files are kept in
\texttt{analysis/paper\_assets/}.

\section{Off-Policy Query-Shift Diagnostic}

Table~\ref{tab:app-offpolicy-shift} gives a local diagnostic for the
off-policy audit route. The audit law is uniform over a selected-query score,
while the deployment law tilts toward high-reward, high-risk queries with
density proportional to $x^{a-1}$. Unweighted auditing estimates the wrong
query law; known-ratio weighting and a hard support cap expose the risk-yield
and effective-sample-size cost.

\begin{table}[!ht]
\centering
\small
\setlength{\tabcolsep}{4pt}
\begin{tabular}{llrrr}
\toprule
Shift & Method & $\tau$ & Test R/Y & Audit ESS \\
\midrule
4 & Unweighted $P$ & 0.895 & 18.65/64.12 & 17910 \\
4 & Weighted $Q/P$ & 0.860 & 4.59/54.67 & 7426 \\
4 & Support cap & 0.850 & 0.00/52.16 & 17038 \\
4 & Oracle $Q$ & 0.860 & 4.59/54.67 & 109340 \\
\midrule
8 & Unweighted $P$ & 0.895 & 33.90/41.19 & 17910 \\
8 & Weighted $Q/P$ & 0.855 & 4.45/28.49 & 3902 \\
8 & Support cap & 0.850 & 0.00/27.23 & 17038 \\
8 & Oracle $Q$ & 0.855 & 4.45/28.49 & 56987 \\
\bottomrule

\caption{Off-policy query-shift toy. Test R/Y is deployment false
